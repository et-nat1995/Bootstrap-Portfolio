About Me

Hi, My name is Tamir Natanov, I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I started programing when I was in highschool, but never really concidered software development as a career choice until later on. After highschool I took a year off from heavy schooling to figure out what exactly I would like to pursue, figure out who I am and what I like. Halfway through that year my parents and i decided to move Israel, still unsure what im doing, I said yes. Let move! I did this to get out of my comfort zone, to leave everything that I had and start life new in a different counrty, state, city. What a better way to move on in life? During my time in Israel I came to a conclusion that I want to pursue a career in the High-Tech industry, Programing. I got accepted into a Bachloers Of Science Degree in Computer Science at The Holon Instute of Technolgy. Toward the end of my first year of three, my father passed away due to sickness. After my father passed away, my mother and I needed to leave, we needed a change. We decided to move to Phoenix. Moving here I wished to conitnue my degree, but the process to transfer all my credits from Israel would take too long. I waited out for a while, but nothing happened. I went back to school taking general classes with some programing classes, which I was told would transfer to ASU, but at the end of the semester, that wasn't the case. At this point I enrolled in Universty of Arizona Full-Stack bootcamp!! Best desicion I made.
